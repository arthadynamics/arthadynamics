About ArthaDynamics

ArthaDynamics is a financial decision tool designed to help individuals better understand the impact of their loan decisions.

Home loans often involve multiple moving parts — interest rate changes, prepayments, and timing decisions. While calculators show numbers, they rarely explain the consequences of these decisions.

ArthaDynamics was built to bridge that gap.

It allows users to simulate different scenarios and understand:

  • How prepayments affect total interest
  • How rate changes impact loan cost
  • How timing decisions influence outcomes
  • How multiple factors interact in non-linear ways

The goal is not to provide advice, but to provide clarity — so users can make more informed decisions.

This is an independent project built with a focus on simplicity, transparency, and practical usefulness.
